Byrd Polar Research Center Archival Program

The Byrd Polar Research Center Archival Program (BPRCAP) is a collaborative effort of the Byrd Polar Research Center and The Ohio State University Libraries. Its mission is to collect, preserve, and provide access to documents, photographs, maps, films, and artifacts concerned with the history of research and exploration in the polar regions.
