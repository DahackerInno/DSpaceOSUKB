Agricultural Technical Institute

Ohio State ATI is located in Wooster, Ohio, and provides educational programs leading to associate degrees in agriculture, horticulture, animal sciences, environmental sciences, business, and engineering technology. Established in 1969, it is a unit of The Ohio State University College of Food, Agricultural, and Environmental Sciences.
