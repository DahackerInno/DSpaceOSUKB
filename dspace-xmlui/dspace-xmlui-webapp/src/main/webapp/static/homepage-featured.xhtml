The National Symposium on Doctoral Research
in Social Work

The National Symposium on Doctoral Research in Social Work showcases doctoral dissertation work in social work from around the country. Presentations are selected via a blind review of submitted abstracts. The Student Poster Presentation, sponsored by the Doctoral Student Organization, provides social work students of all degree levels with an opportunity to present and share their research.
