The Steven Enich Serbian Orthodox Culture Slide Collection

The Enich collection consists of almost 5,000 slide photographs taken by amateur photographer Steven Enich between 1979 and 1994. A lawyer and philanthropist, Mr. Enich was given unprecedented access to Serbian Orthodox cultural monuments in the former Yugoslavia, some of which are no longer in existence.
