The OSU International Symposium on Molecular Spectroscopy

The OSU International Symposium on Molecular Spectroscopy had its 67th consecutive annual meeting in June 2012. The subjects covered at the meeting include all experimental and theoretical aspects of rotational, vibrational, and electronic spectroscopy of molecules in the gas phase or in other environments providing relevant spectra. Also included are applications of molecular spectroscopy in related fields such as physics, chemistry, atmospheric science, astronomy, remote sensing and so on.
